# SkyDelay-Intelligence: Business Brief

## Executive Summary
SkyDelay-Intelligence is a comprehensive platform designed to analyze flight delays and quantify their economic impact in real-time. By integrating data from OpenSky Network, FAA, and NOAA, the system provides actionable insights for airlines, airports, and insurers.

## Key Value Propositions
1.  **Real-time Monitoring**: Live tracking of flight positions and airport status.
2.  **Economic Quantification**: precise estimation of delay costs including passenger compensation, crew overtime, and fuel burn.
3.  **Cascade Analysis**: Understanding how a single delay ripples through the network.
4.  **AI-Driven Insights**: Agentic interface for natural language queries about network health.

## Target Audience
- **Airline Operations Managers**: Optimize crew scheduling and aircraft utilization.
- **Airport Authorities**: Improve ground resource allocation.
- **Travel Insurers**: Better risk assessment and automated claims processing.
